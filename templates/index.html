Welcome to Sweets Café!

Sweets Café is a cozy Asian patisserie that focuses on handcrafted desserts and specialty drinks made with care and attention to detail. We serve favorites including classic mantou, rich snowflake crisps, and smooth matcha lattes made with high-quality matcha.

Some of our signature drinks include the banana cream matcha latte and the cream-top matcha latte, balancing sweetness and earthy flavors in a comforting way.
